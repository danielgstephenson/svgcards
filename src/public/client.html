Do each phase together as a group

Phase 1 - Plan

- Exile 1 card in your hand face down behind name tag
- Play 1 card from your hand face down
- Draw from deck or take pawns until you have at least 3 in hand

Phase 2 - Eyes

- Reveal play cards simultaneously
- If total eyes on played cards is more than player count, put lowest rank center card on trial

Phase 3 - Powers

- Use listed powers on card you played

Phase 4 - Arrest

- Put highest rank played card on trial, ties go to the dungeon
- If center is empty, game ends

Phase 5 - Auction

- Announce bonus powers on played cards
- Raise bid by any amount of gold any time
- Withdraw if tied and not last bidder
- When no one wants to, highest untied bidder takes lowest rank center card into discard

Phase 6 - Discard

- Buyer may take any cards on trial into discard, remaining go to dungeon
- Discard played card
- Cards in discard enter deck from lowest to highest

Victory

- Points equals rank of cards in hand and play
- 1 point for gold and silver
- No points for cards in deck
- Tiebreaker is highest card in hand
